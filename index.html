Kozhikode is one of the main commercial cities of Kerala. The economy is mainly business oriented. The city currently is the major trade hub of North Kerala with good connectivity through road, rail and air. It also has large timber yards along the banks of the Kallayi River. Kozhikode District, with 8% of the state population, makes 12% contribution to the state's income. There has been a great leap in small-scale industries in Kozhikode district. Footwear industries have been flourishing in this city, and it has become a major non-leather footwear manufacturing hub in India. Industries of almost every business are located in Kozhikode. The varieties ranges from mineral water producer to compressor manufacturer, material handling, wire netting, pipes, oil and essential, fiber glass producer, latex processing, etc. The most important among them are Grasim Industries Ltd, Simco Food Industries, Videocon Industries Ltd, J K Tyre & Industry LTD. Titan Industries Limited, etc. Most of these important industries are located in the SEZ and industrial area. The popular areas that are known for industries in Kozhikode are Feroke, Karapparambu, Nadakkavu, Kasargod, Kannur Chettikadavu and Cheruvannur.
